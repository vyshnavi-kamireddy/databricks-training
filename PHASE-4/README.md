# 📈 Business Tasks Completed

## ✅ Task 1 – Daily Sales

Calculated the total sales generated each day.

**Output**

- Order Date
- Total Sales

---

## ✅ Task 2 – City-wise Revenue

Calculated total revenue generated by customers from each city.

**Output**

- City
- Total Revenue

---

## ✅ Task 3 – Top 5 Customers

Identified the top five customers based on total spending.

**Output**

- Customer Name
- Total Spend

---

## ✅ Task 4 – Repeat Customers

Identified customers who placed more than one order.

**Output**

- Customer ID
- Order Count

---

## ✅ Task 5 – Customer Segmentation

Customers were categorized into business segments based on total spending.

| Segment | Condition |
|----------|-----------|
| 🥇 Gold | Total Spend > 10000 |
| 🥈 Silver | 5000 – 10000 |
| 🥉 Bronze | Less than 5000 |

---

## ✅ Task 6 – Final Reporting Table

Generated a consolidated business report containing:

- Customer Name
- City
- Total Spend
- Order Count
- Customer Segment
